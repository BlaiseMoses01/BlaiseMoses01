## At Work 
 
At my 9-5, most of my current work is centered around **RAG system architecture & evaluation, Model Context Protocol Server management and orchestration, and internal AI enablement tooling**. I was also recently recognized internally as a Claude Code and Software Quality Champion/SME, and have became a direct partner with leadership supporting org-wide enablement.
 
Most of my professional work falls into one of three buckets:
 
- **Inference Guardrails and Infrastructure** — I build evaluation harnesses, merge-gate pipelines, and feedback loops to automate the analytics layer around model quality and also create guardrails that catch hallucinations in-loop and correct to reduce cycle times, save token resources, and drive models towards more deterministic outcomes.

- **Agentic Architecture Strategy and Implementation** — I bounce around internal teams, work with key contributors to understand their workflows and assess automation potential, and then design and implement agentic solutions to suit their constraints and accelerate their velocity while also optimizing for cost and scalability.

- **Mentorship and Enablement** — I work to upskill teams of varying technical backgrounds to leverage AI tools effectively in their day to day work, and am a frequent keynote speaker in AI training seminars and strategy meetings. I also directly interview and train junior AI Engineers and SDETs and help them strengthen fundamental engineering skills and reach their career goals.
